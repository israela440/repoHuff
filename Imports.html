As you may already know our coffee comes in three different varieties:

  • Bourbon
  • Typica
  • Caturra

All of these coffee plants are growned and sourced from Coatepec, Veracruz in Mexico where all of our products are made. The things we value most with our products are;

  1. Affordability
  2. Quality
  3. Taste
  4. Texture
  5. Customization
  6. Memorability

The fouder of The Birb Beans while not born in Coatepec, grew up in the magic town, now you might wondering, what's a magic town? Well in Veracruz, the state Coatepec is in, magic town or more know as "Pueblos Magicos" are tourist destination marked out by the state. Coatepec is one of the best destinations for a vacation due to not only its ideal geographic location and beautiful weather, but more than anything the coffee. In the parks vendors will set up shop and sell coffee related products that we praomise are cheaper than the ones we sell. Not only that but the quality of the coffee is ten times better there than if it was to travel all the way to your home. So please come on down to Coatepec, Veracruz to enjoy a nice warm cup of coffee when you wake up.
